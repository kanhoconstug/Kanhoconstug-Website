About Us

"Your vision is our blueprint. We are the premier construction partner that transforms complex ideas into exceptional, buildable reality, on time and on budget."

Building Dreams Since 2020

Kanho Construction Ltd. has established itself as a premier construction company known for delivering exceptional quality and unparalleled craftsmanship. Since its inception, the company has evolved through the leadership of its founder, who has undertaken a range of challenging projects, gaining extensive expertise in:

  • Architectural Design
  • Construction & Renovation
  • Building Maintenance
  • Project Management
  • Engineering Services

Over the years, Kanho Construction Ltd has built a reputation for delivering practical, cost- effective, and well-managed solutions tailored to diverse project needs since we believe that exceptional spaces are born from a seamless union of thoughtful design and expert craftsmanship. As a full-service design and build company, we are committed to transforming ideas into lasting, livable realities managing every step of the process with precision, creativity, and care. Whether it's a contemporary home, a commercial workspace, or a bespoke renovation, our team blends innovation with function to build environments that not only serve but inspire. We work closely with our clients to bring their vision to life.

    Graduate Training Program

    Launch Your Career with a Solid Foundation

    The Kanho Construction Graduate Training Program is a structured, 24-month rotational program designed to transform recent graduates into the future leaders of our company. You will gain exposure to various departments, including project management, site operations, estimating, and safety, all while receiving mentorship from industry veterans.

    Program Highlights:

    • Four 6-month rotations across different business units
    • Dedicated mentor and program manager
    • Formal training in leadership, communication, and technical skills
    • Responsibility for real projects with measurable outcomes
    • A clear pathway to a permanent role upon successful completion

    Who We're Looking For:

    • Recent graduates (within the last 12 months) with a Bachelor's or Master's degree in Construction Management, Civil Engineering, or a related field
    • Demonstrated leadership potential and academic excellence
    • Strong problem-solving and analytical skills
    • Adaptability and a strong desire to build a long-term career at Kanho

    Internship Program

    Gain Real-World Experience on the Front Lines

    Our internship program is designed for students currently enrolled in construction management, engineering, architecture, or related trade programs. This is not a "get coffee" internship; you will be integrated into our project teams, contributing to live projects and gaining invaluable hands-on experience.

    What You'll Do:

    • Assist project managers and superintendents with daily site operations
    • Review project plans and documents
    • Participate in site safety meetings and inspections
    • Support quality control and assurance processes
    • Learn to use industry-standard software and project management tools

    Who We're Looking For:

    • Students currently pursuing a degree or diploma in a construction-related field
    • Individuals with a strong desire to learn and a proactive attitude
    • Excellent communication and teamwork skills
    • A keen focus on safety and attention to detail
